Hi, glad you stopped by! I'm Florin — scientist, musician, and photographer. I currently live in the beautiful city of Heidelberg and am in the final stretch of my PhD.

I've been taking photos since my teenage years, but only in recent years has it grown into a serious and beloved hobby. Portrait photography is what excites me most right now. I photograph primarily for myself and for the people in the pictures, which is why you won't find me on social media and I often end up just printing my photos and hanging them on the wall or making photo books. If you'd like to step in front of the camera and work with me, feel free to reach out via the collaboration section!
